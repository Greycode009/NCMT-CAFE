About Us

A calm café for coffee, chiya, and local comfort food.

NCMT Café is a cozy Nepali-style café where people come to relax, enjoy freshly brewed drinks, and taste local favorites in a peaceful space.

Fresh Drinks

Coffee, cappuccino, and traditional Nepali chiya.

Local Taste

Momo, sel roti, chowmein, and café snacks.

Calm Space

A quiet, friendly place to relax and spend time.

Our mission is simple — serve quality food and drinks in a welcoming environment.
